  1. First round consists of MCQ which has 30 minutes as the time limit.

  2. This Round consists of three levels which are Easy, Medium & Hard.
    Each easy level question is for 2 marks,Medium level question is for 4 marks,Hard level question is for 8 marks.

  3. Each correct question will take you to higher level & Each wrong question will bring you to previous level.

  4. There is negative marking for all questions.
    (+2,-1) for easy level question,(+4,-2) for medium level question, (+8,-4) for hard level question.

  5. There are 2 lifelines in this level.
    You can skip atmost 1 question if you don't know the answer and after answering 3 correct questions ,The skip option will get enabled automatically.

  6. There is an option for BONUS which can be used only once.
    Clicking on that option will let you score the marks in following ways.
    Marking scheme for different types of question are:-
    i. For easy level: (+4,-2)
    ii. For Medium level: (+8,-4)
    iii. For hard level: (+10,-8)
